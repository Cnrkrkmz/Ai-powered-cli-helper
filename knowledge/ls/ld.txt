# ls — ld examples

Task: Prints long listing of directories '/tmp', '/tnt' themselves bordered with '<--' and '--->'.
Command: ls -ld /tmp /tnt | sed 's/^.*$/<-- & --->/'

Task: Prints long listing of directories '/tmp', '/tnt' themselves.
Command: ls -ld /tmp /tnt

Task: Recursively lists all files in a current folder in long format.
Command: ls -ld $(find .)
