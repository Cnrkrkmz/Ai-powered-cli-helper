# stanctl — License management

[license-download]
Downloads the Instana license file.
Usage: stanctl license download [--sales-key <key>] [-o <dir>]
Alias: stanctl lc
Output: -o, --output-dir (default: /root/.stanctl/license.json)

[license-info]
Displays information about the current license validity.
Usage: stanctl license info

[license-update]
Updates the license, download key, and sales key.
Usage: stanctl license update [flags]
Online: --download-key and/or --sales-key
Air-gapped (from file): -f, --file <license-file>
