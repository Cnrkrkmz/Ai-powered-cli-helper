# rsync — nvral examples

Task: Perform a dry run to recursively copy "test/a" to "test/dest" excluding "test/a/b/c/d"
Command: rsync -nvraL test/a test/dest --exclude=a/b/c/d

Task: Recursively archive "test/a/" to "test/dest" excluding "test/a/b/c/d"
Command: rsync -nvraL test/a/ test/dest --exclude=/b/c/d
